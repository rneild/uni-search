## Notes

Research date: 2026-03-23. Sources: Union College official site, US News, Niche reviews, SavvyPreMed, LittleIvyColleges guide.

### The LIM Programme
The Leadership in Medicine programme is the most distinctive thing about Union for a pre-med applicant. It is a direct-entry, guaranteed 8-year pathway to an MD via Albany Medical College — no MCAT required as long as cumulative GPA and math/science GPA stay above 3.50. Only ~15 students are admitted per cohort. Requirements: SAT 1410+ or ACT 30+, and US citizen or permanent resident status. If she qualifies and decides to commit to medicine early, this is worth serious consideration. If she wants the flexibility to explore first (her current stated preference), the standard pre-med track with the Early Assurance Programme is the better fit.

### Albany Medical College Early Assurance
Non-LIM Union students can apply to Albany Medical College at the end of sophomore year. If accepted, they proceed to Union for two more years then enter AMC — bypassing the standard MCAT/application cycle. This is a meaningful safety net for a motivated pre-med student.

### Academic Calendar Note
The trimester system is unusual. Three 10-week terms: September to Thanksgiving, January to March, April to mid-June. The late spring end date (mid-June) is later than most peer schools. Students typically take three courses per term (not five like semester schools) — intensity per course is high.
